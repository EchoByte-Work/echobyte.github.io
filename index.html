Revisions & Design

3.1 Included Revision Rounds

3.1.1 The Client is entitled to two (2) included revision rounds during the preview stage described in clause 2.3.

3.1.2 A revision round means a consolidated list of major design, content, or functionality changes submitted by the Client in one go, and not individual change requests sent one at a time.

3.1.3 Minor corrections — such as spelling mistakes, broken links, implementation errors, and bugs — do not count as revision rounds and will be corrected by EchoByte as part of the build, at no additional charge.

3.1.4 Revision rounds do not include complete redesigns or changes to the original project brief.

3.2 Additional Revisions

3.2.1 Any major revisions requested after the two included rounds will be charged at R250 per additional revision round, and will be added to the Client's final invoice.

3.2.2 Additional revision charges will be quoted and agreed in writing before any further work begins, and must be settled, together with the setup fee, before the website is launched.

3.3.1 EchoByte shall make every effort to deliver a design that is consistent with the Client's brief. Any dissatisfaction with the initial design direction must be communicated in writing during the first revision round.

3.3.2 Where the Client remains dissatisfied after both included revision rounds have been used, any further changes will be treated, and invoiced, as additional revisions in terms of clause 3.2.

3.3.3 No refunds shall be issued for completed work. EchoByte's obligation is to apply the included revision rounds specified in this agreement, and no further obligation arises once those rounds have been exhausted.

3.4.1 All website change requests must be submitted to Echobyte via email, including a clear description of the requested changes and any supporting content.

3.4.2 Echobyte will use reasonable endeavours to implement approved change requests. Changes will be published no later than the 5th of the month following receipt of the request. Urgent changes requiring implementation within 24 hours will attract an additional charge of R80, which must be agreed upon in writing prior to commencement.

3.4.3 On a monthly basis, Echobyte will issue a written communication to the Client requesting any pending change requests, together with a status update on the Client's website, including uptime status and any revisions implemented during that period.

Tip: The level of detail provided by the Client in the initial brief is directly correlated with the number of revision rounds required. Clients are strongly advised to provide comprehensive style references, colour preferences, and content examples at the outset.
